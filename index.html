FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know about our events and services.

Contact Support
What types of events do you host?

We host a wide variety of events including technology conferences, business summits, industry exhibitions, trade shows, workshops, and hybrid virtual-in-person events. Our platform supports events of all sizes, from intimate workshops to large-scale conventions.

How do I register for an event?

Registration is simple! Browse our events, select the one you're interested in, choose your ticket type, and complete the checkout process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your digital ticket and event details.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, we offer flexible refund policies. Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Between 7-30 days, you'll receive a 50% refund or credit toward a future event. Refunds are not available within 7 days, but you can transfer your ticket.

Do you offer virtual attendance options?

Absolutely! Many of our events offer hybrid experiences. Virtual attendees can access live streams of keynote sessions, participate in Q&A, visit virtual exhibition booths, and network through our dedicated platform. Virtual tickets are available at a reduced price.

How can I become a speaker?

We're always looking for passionate speakers! Submit your proposal through our Speaker Application page. Include your bio, topic idea, previous speaking experience, and why your content would be valuable to our audience.
