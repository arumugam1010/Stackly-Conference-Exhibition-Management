Pricing FAQ

Payment & Billing Questions

Are there any commissions on tickets checkout? +

No. We believe in keeping ticketing pricing clean and simple. Unlike other platforms, we do NOT take commission margins or transactional cuts from ticket purchases. You pay only the flat monthly/annual subscription fee.

Can I downgrade or cancel my subscription plan? +

Absolutely. You can cancel or change your plan level directly inside your organizer admin dashboard settings panel at any time. There are no locking periods or cancellation fees.
