FAQ

Service Guidelines & Questions

Can you customize templates to fit our brand guidelines? +

Absolutely. Our Pro and Enterprise plan features allow custom logo uploads, personalized email structures, customized ticket formats, and complete white-label staging options.

What latency standards do you maintain for virtual webcasting? +

We utilize low-latency HTTP live streaming channels (HLS/WebRTC), maintaining stream latencies under 2-3 seconds globally, ensuring real-time Q&A feedback is fully interactive.

Do you provide dedicated physical staff on-site? +

Yes, our Enterprise packages include on-site event managers, network coordination specialists, check-in desk AV technicians, and support crews to handle hardware desks and routing desks.
